Snowboarder

May 18, 2016 Amy H

For this snowboarder flash assignment, we had to extract a snowboarder of our choice on photoshop then put the final png into flash. I learned that if you want to import a file onto flash, you have to use "export" instead of "open". I also learned how to use the onion skin tool. The onion skin tool was very helpful for looking at my previous adjustments.
